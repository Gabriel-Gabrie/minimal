Welcome to

Minimal

Let’s walk through the basics so you can start managing your money in minutes.

Swipe or tap → to begin

Step 1

Set Up Your Wallet

Add your real accounts — spending, saving, and debt.
This lets you track where your money lives and moves between accounts.

Step 2

Create a Budget

Go to the Budgets tab and set monthly spending limits for each category.
Bars glow green, amber, or red so you can see your status at a glance.

Step 3

Add Transactions

Tap the + button to log income, expenses, or transfers between accounts.
Pick a category, enter the amount, and save.

Manage Your History

All your transactions appear in a searchable, filterable list.
Tap any row to see details or edit.
Swipe left to delete.

Your Overview

Your home tab shows this month’s spending vs. budget with a pace indicator.
See top categories and recent transactions at a glance.

Settings

Tap your profile icon (top-right) to open Settings.
Switch themes, export your data as a backup, or import from another device.

You’re all set!

Start by adding your accounts, then set a budget. You can always replay this tour from Settings.
